About Me

I am Catalina Lai, and what definds me is my past, present, and future. I come from a family with strong beliefs, traditions, and cultures. I am the youngest out of eight brothers and sisters who have all grounded me, and made me the person I am today.

I was raised Catholic, and on traditional strong Vietnamese/Asian ideals and standards. School was priority after God, and that nothing was better than an "A". The only fields excepted was: Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math.

I am Catalina Lai. I am traditional. I am untraditional. I am an artist. I am sarcastic. I am a nerd. I am a hardworker. I AM A CODER.
